About

I'm an artist from Palo Alto, California, who makes art inspired by landscapes and species local to places special to me: my home in the San Francisco Bay Area, Australia, and the corners of my imagination.

Each design is created by hand in pencil, ink, watercolor, acrylic, or digital brushstrokes.

No AI is used anywhere in my work, not even in building this website.

My cards are printed locally and shipped in sustainable paper mailers.

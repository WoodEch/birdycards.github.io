About

I'm an artist from Palo Alto, California, who is inspired by the landscapes and species of places special to me: my home in the San Francisco Bay Area, Australia, and the corners of my imagination.

Each design is created by hand in pencil, ink, watercolor, acrylic, or digital brushstrokes. No AI is used anywhere in my work, not even in building this website. (If you find something broken, use the Contact form to let me know!)

My cards are printed in Palo Alto and shipped in sustainable paper mailers.

Visit my Etsy Shop to order or browse designs below.
